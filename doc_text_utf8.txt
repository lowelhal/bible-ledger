Bible Tracking App – Project Overview & Requirements
Introduction: An Overview of the Bible Tracking App
As Christians, we know the importance of studying the Word of God, yet many of us struggle to complete structured reading plans, such as “read the Bible in a year.” Life gets in the way, schedules shift, and our intentions don’t always align with reality. However, even if you don't complete a formal plan, you likely engage with Scripture more often than you realize. A passage during a devotional, a reading in church, group Bible study discussions, or a verse of the day—these moments add up over time.
What if, throughout your Christian walk, you've already read most—or even all—of the Bible without knowing it? Perhaps there are just a few books or chapters you’ve yet to explore. Wouldn't it be valuable to track your Bible reading, whether from a structured plan or spontaneous encounters? Imagine being able to reflect on how much Scripture you've covered in the past month, year, or several years. You could discover which passages you return to most often and identify areas you’ve yet to explore.
We live in a time where tracking progress is common: we monitor our physical activity, nutrition, travel, and even mental well-being. So, why not track our time in the Bible too? 
There are many great resources out there to help, 
but none have ticked all the boxes for me. 
Many focus solely on structured plans or require manual tracking without providing an easy way to record spontaneous readings. None offer a complete view of your Scripture engagement across multiple contexts.
Introducing the Bible Tracking System
The Bible Tracking System provides a unified platform that allows users to track their Scripture reading across various contexts, encouraging meaningful engagement at their own pace. This app combines tracking, statistics, achievements, and personal notes into a seamless experience, helping users reflect on their spiritual growth over time.
This can serve as a comprehensive hub that accommodates everything from devotionals and structured study plans to casual readings and group studies. It provides options that can remove the friction of switching between apps or manually logging progress, giving users a clear, insightful view of their Bible journey.
The Bible Tracking System introduces several key features that streamline Bible engagement, offering both flexibility and a comprehensive overview of users' progress.

Backend API Overview
Bible Reading API
 (Internal Only):
GET /bible/{translation}/{book}/{chapter}
: Retrieve chapter text.
GET /bible/translations
: List all available translations.
POST /bible/download-kjv
: Initiate KJV download for offline use.
Progress Tracking API
:
POST /progress
: Add a new progress entry.
GET /progress
: Retrieve progress by user or session.
PUT /progress/{id}
: Update an existing progress entry.
DELETE /progress/{id}
: Delete a progress entry.
Highlights API
:
POST /highlights
: Add a new highlight.
GET /highlights
: Retrieve highlights by user, book, or chapter.
PUT /highlights/{id}:
 Update an existing highlight.
DELETE /highlights/{id}
: Remove a highlight.
Notes API
:
POST /notes
: Add a note to a verse.
GET /notes
: Retrieve notes by user or passage.
PUT /notes/{id}
: Update an existing note.
DELETE /notes/{id}
: Delete a note.
API Key Management
: Managed externally
